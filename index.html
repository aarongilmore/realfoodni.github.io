Introduction

The food and drink culture in Northern Ireland has shifted a lot in recent years. For a long time a lack of care and consideration was given to the food we served. Now, people who care deeply—not just about food—but about the community surrounding it have taken the food and drink culture in Northern Ireland to a new level. It’s wonderful.
