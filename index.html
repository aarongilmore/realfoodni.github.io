Introduction

The food and drink culture in Northern Ireland has shifted a lot in recent years. For a long time people were content with serving and consuming subpar food. Now, people who care deeply, not just about food, but about the community surrounding it have taken the food and drink culture in Northern Ireland to a new level.

This is a curated list of some of my favourite places to eat and drink in the country. Know somewhere that you think should be on the list? Email me.
